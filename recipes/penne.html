Penne à la bolognese

Description

This is a simple, yet delicious pasta recipe.

This was my comfort food when I was a child. My yaya used to cook this for me and my cousin everytime we visited.

Disclaimer: I am aware this is not a traditional italian Penne à la Bolognese, this is just how my Spanish grandma did it

Ingredients

  • Penne....500g
  • Grounded beef....500g
  • 1 Onion
  • Tomato sauce....500g
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Mince and frie the onion with a bit of olive oil
  2. Once the onion is golden, add the grounded beef
  3. Add the tomato sauce
  4. Add salt and sugar to taste
  5. In the meantime, boil some water and cook the pasta
  6. Finally, drain the pasta and add the sauce you just made
  7. You can add some grated cheese
  8. Enjoy!
