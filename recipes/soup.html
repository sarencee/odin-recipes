Classic chicken broth soup

Description

This is the classic Spanish chicken broth soup

I love soup. I love all kinds of soups. But this one is close to my heart.

My mother is the master of chicken broth soup, and this is how she does it:

Ingredients

  • Chicken carcass....1
  • Chicken rump....2
  • Leek....1
  • Carrot....1
  • Parsley sprigs....4
  • Parsnip....1
  • Ham bones (optional, can make the broth very salty)....1

Steps

  1. Put a large pot with water to boil
  2. Add some salt and olive oil to the water
  3. Clean the carcass anda dd it to the water
  4. Add the chicken rumps to the water
  5. Peel the vegetables and cut them, then add them to the pot
  6. Lower the heat and let it cook for 2-3 hours
  7. Check regularly, so the water doesn't evaporate too much and you are left with very few broth (it happened to me)/li>
  8. When th ebroth is ready, retire the bones and vegetables and strain
  9. You can leave the chicken rumps
  10. Cook come soup pasta in the broth
  11. You can freeze the rest of the broth, I usually make enough for 6-8 servings
